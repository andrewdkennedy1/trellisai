TrellisAI is an AI farm assistant that turns everyday field notes into tomorrow's work plan.

The demo starts with realistic daily notes: pests, irrigation pressure, crop stress, labor shortages, equipment delays, and weather risk. The log history looks like notes a grower or scout would actually write.

When a new scouting note is saved, Trellis pulls out the important field signals and adds them to farm memory.

On the next turn, the grower asks what to do tomorrow. Trellis shows what it checked: recent field risks, similar past notes, and unfinished jobs.

The explanation stays short and practical. The grower can approve a plan item, and Trellis turns it into an open field job.
